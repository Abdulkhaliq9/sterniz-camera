Academy

achkundeprüfung nach §34a GewO

The Sachkundeprüfung nach §34a GewO is a mandatory certification for individuals working in the security industry in Germany.

Unterrichtung nach §34a GewO

The Unterrichtung nach §34a GewO is an introductory training for individuals entering the security field without needing to pass an exam. It covers basic legal knowledge, safety standards, and practical aspects of security work.

Waffensachkunde

The Waffensachkunde course provides the necessary knowledge and qualifications for individuals working with firearms in the security industry.
