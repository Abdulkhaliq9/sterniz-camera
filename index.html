The Digital Twin Token (DTT) is a unique digital asset backed by comprehensive property data. Our service integrates your building’s components and essential system information into a cohesive DTT.

CONNECT THE BUILDING'S TWIN TO YOUR STAKEHOLDERS

Users connect directly to the DTT without any intermediaries. As Stakeholder, they have the power to upload and verify information that enriches the Digital Twin Token. A Stakeholder's ability to access validated-data to perform tasks depends on their specific role.

Academy

Sachkundeprüfung nach §34a GewO

The Sachkundeprüfung nach §34a GewO is a mandatory certification for individuals working in the security industry in Germany.

Unterrichtung nach §34a GewO

The Unterrichtung nach §34a GewO is an introductory training for individuals entering the security field without needing to pass an exam. It covers basic legal knowledge, safety standards, and practical aspects of security work.

Waffensachkunde

The Waffensachkunde course provides the necessary knowledge and qualifications for individuals working with firearms in the security industry.
